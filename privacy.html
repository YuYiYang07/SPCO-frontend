1. Who Are We

The Singapore Polytechnic Chinese Orchestra ( hereafter referred to as SPCO ) is a student co-curricular activity (CCA) club operating under Singapore Polytechnic. SPCO is a non-profit, student-run organisation and does not engage in commercial activities, advertising, or data monetisation.

This Privacy Statement sets out how SPCO collects, uses, and safeguards personal data submitted through our website and enrolment forms, in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA) of Singapore.

2. Data We Collect

When you submit our enrolment form, we may collect the following personal data:

  • Full name
  • Singapore Polytechnic Student ID
  • Mobile phone number
  • Musical instrument and years of experience
  • Sectional interest
  • Any additional information you voluntarily provide

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data such as NRIC numbers, financial information, health data, or biometric identifiers. Users are advised not to submit such information.

SPCO reserves the right to delete any data deemed irrelevant, offensive, or in violation of Singapore Polytechnic’s Student Code of Conduct.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your personal data may be used for the following purposes:

  • Processing and administering your CCA enrolment
  • Communicating with you regarding auditions, rehearsals, and administrative matters
  • Maintaining internal records for operational and organisational purposes
  • Facilitating sectional allocation based on your instrument and experience

Your data will not be used for marketing or commercial purposes. SPCO does not sell, rent, or monetise personal data.

4. Data Storage

Personal data is stored using third-party services, including Google Sheets and Google Apps Script, and transmitted over secure HTTPS connections.

While reasonable security measures are implemented, SPCO does not control and is not responsible for the security practices of third-party service providers.

SPCO reserves the right to migrate data to alternative storage systems or platforms as operational needs evolve. This Privacy Statement may be updated accordingly.

5. Who Can Access Your Data

Access to personal data is restricted to authorised SPCO Executive Committee (Exco) members on a need-to-know basis.

SPCO does not disclose personal data to third parties except:

  • Where required by Singapore Polytechnic policies or administrative processes
  • Where required or permitted by applicable law or regulatory authorities

6. Data Retention

Personal data may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this statement, or as required by applicable laws and institutional policies.

In general:

  • Member data may be retained for a period deemed reasonably necessary (typically up to 3 years)
  • Data of applicants who do not join or who withdraw may be deleted within a reasonable period

Notwithstanding the above, SPCO reserves the right to retain limited information relating to past Exco members, section leaders, or principal performers for archival, historical, and organisational record purposes, including display on official platforms.

7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to personal data held about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Withdraw consent for the collection and use of your data

SPCO reserves the right to verify the identity of requestors and may decline requests where permitted under applicable law.

Please Note : Withdrawing consent may result in SPCO being unable to process your enrolment or maintain your active membership.

9. PROTECTION OF MINORS

We acknowledge that applicants may be under the age of 18. By submitting this form, you represent that you have obtained the necessary parental or guardian consent to provide your personal data to SPCO.

SPCO does not collect data from individuals under the age of 18 (escpecially under 13) if we really do know that his/her parents disagree with this statement.

10. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

This Privacy Statement is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of Singapore. Regardless of the user's nationality or physical location, all data processing activities are conducted within the jurisdiction of Singapore.

International students with non-singapore nationalities acknowledge that their data protection standards will be aligned with the Singapore PDPA, Although this may differ from the regulations in their home countries (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

11. Changes to This Statement

SPCO reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Statement at any time without prior notice. Changes will take effect immediately upon publication on this page.

Continued use of this website or submission of personal data constitutes your acknowledgment and acceptance of the revised terms.

12. Consent

By submitting any personal data through this website or related forms, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Statement and consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal data as described herein.

Where applicable, you represent that the information provided is accurate and that you have the authority to provide such data.
